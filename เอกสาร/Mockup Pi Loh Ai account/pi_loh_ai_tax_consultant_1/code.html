How should I handle disallowed expenses for my SME's corporate tax return? Specifically about travel receipts.

smart_toy

Under the Thai Revenue Code, disallowed expenses for Corporate Income Tax (CIT) are governed primarily by Section 65 Ter.

For travel receipts to be deductible:
1. They must be directly related to the business.
2. You must have a proper receipt (Tax Invoice/Receipt).
3. Expenses without a clear recipient are disallowed under Section 65 Ter (18).
